The Studio

Hidden Oaks Cottage is a magnificent place for women to relax and rejuvenate, enjoy each other’s company, and take advantage of some ‘just for you’ time.


"You have done a beautiful job furnishing, decorating and equipping Hidden Oaks Cottage for comfort, and convenience. There was numerous places close by to order take out as well as shop if we forgot something. The fridge space was ample and very clean. Everyone loved resting in the beautiful comfortable beds. The view of the woods created a warm inviting environment in which to let our creative juices flow."

-Jeannie P., Creative Memories Consultant, Prior Lake, MN


You deserve some time to re-group and work on the craft that brings you and your family joy.

During the course of your stay we have arranged for the necessities to be ready for you before you arrive. All you need to bring is your personal supplies and favorite beverages/snacks. The cottage is easy to access and no steps in your way. This results in bringing in supplies and suitcases a breeze and you can start crafting right away.

The cottage is nestled in a 3 acre dense wooded lot in the suburbs which makes it very convenient for you to leave and purchase supplies should you forget something. Lakeville (Target 2 miles), Burnsville, and Shakopee (Michael's, JoAnn's ) are all within 10 to 18 minute easy commute. The cottage is easily accessable and it is very easy to get to. You can invest more money in buying craft supplies versus having to fill-up the gas tank to get away for a crafting weekend.

The three comfortable twin beds will be made up with fresh linens and the 1.5 full bathrooms will be stocked with towels and washcloths. A fourth guest is welcome to the roll out cot.
