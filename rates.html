Standard Rates

Friday & Saturday night stay with optional add on nights Thursday or Sunday

Check-in is at Noon on Friday.
Check-out is at 3:00pm on Sunday

Please call or e-mail me if you like to arrange for earlier or later Check-in and Check-out times. I will try to accommodate the best I can. Minimum of $320 a weekend. Weekend is considered a Fri. a.m. check-in & a Sunday early p.m. check-out.
1 Guest = $320 plus tax (Sorry, I can not take a reservation for 1 person for the weekend, unless you are willing to pay the minimum weekend rate of $220 plus MN. tax)
2 Guest = $320 plus tax
3 Guest = $320 plus tax
4 Guest = $425 plus tax
The tax is the MN current sales tax at 6.875%.

Friends and Families can stop in to see what you are working on at no additional charge.
If they decide to stay the night. Please pay the per person one night rate of $55.
The Retreat comfortably sleeps four (4) adults (three (3) Twin Beds and (1) Roll out cot with thicker cushion and fluffy down comforter). Days and nights such as Thursday and Sunday are available for add on days.
Two night minimum stay is required.

Payment is asked in full the day of check-in.
The $50 reservation deposit will be held as a security deposit incase of excessive damage. This is very rare that damage occurs. I have the "Best Customer Base". After check out I will clean and service the rental. If all is good, I will shred the $50 reservation/deposit check and notify you by e-mail, v-mail or text.

Add Thursday or Sunday evening *$30 per person

We can accommodate families in town for funerals, weddings, sporting events. Music festival, any large city event. Day rentals also available. Please contact us for special pricing and accommodations.

Minimum two guests for add-on nights. Rate does not include tax. Extra days must coincide with a weekend booking. Pricing is based on a per person and a per night stay.

Other Housekeeping Business

Accidents happen; if something gets broken, please call us or leave a note so the item can be fixed for our next guests. Should a plumbing, heating or A/C repair be needed, please contact us immediately. Renters will be responsible for any excessive damage to the property, the cottage or its contents. The cottage is yours for the duration of your stay, treat it as such. In the event of property damage a detailed list and an invoice for the repairs will be sent to the renter payable upon receipt.
