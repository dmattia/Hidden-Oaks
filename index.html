Welcome!

Enjoy a weekend relaxing with family & friends, while preserving memories and creating new ones in this beautiful cottage nestled on a tranquil, three acre wooded area in Prior Lake, MN. Hidden Oaks Cottage is sure to please any crafter.

Furnished with three twin beds and a fourth pull out bed as well as one and half full bathrooms. The retreat has ample space for three to four guests. Includes all towels, bed linens and plenty of room to Shutterfly/Scrapbook or Craft. The craft room has all the necessities and a few extras! It has six roomy workstations, a snack bar with a full kitchen, a fire place to relax in front of, outside patio with a view of a pond, a stereo, flat screen televisions synced to a DVD player so you can catch up on new releases or classic oldies during your retreat. Hidden Oaks Cottage is conveniently located near a winery that host weekend tasting tours in the Fall and main street Prior Lake. Visit the links below of the other near by attractions.
